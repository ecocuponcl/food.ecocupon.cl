♻️EcoCupon

Digital infrastructure for circular economy — turning waste into cashback through QR validation, anti-fraud systems, and sovereign wallets.

🔥 "Tus clientes reciben dinero real por reciclar lo que compran."

EcoCupon is a vertical implementation of the SmarterBOT framework. It provides businesses with payment integration (Flow.cl), recycling validation, and automatic cashback — all running on local-first infrastructure.

Functional Workflow

  1. 🛒 Purchase — Customer buys via Flow.cl payment gateway
  2. 📦 QR Generation — Each product gets a unique QR code: ECO-{timestamp}-{uuid} with HMAC signature
  3. 📷 Scan — Customer scans QR on packaging after consumption
  4. 🔍 Validation — Multi-factor: image SHA256 hash, GPS zone, min delay hours
  5. 🚛 Confirmation — Truck/drop-point confirms physical pickup
  6. 💰 Cashback — Credits deposited in eco.wallet — usable in-store or withdrawable

eco.wallet

Internal wallet separated from Flow.cl — cashback is credited here, Flow.cl only used for withdrawals.

💡 Design Decision: Flow.cl is used only for payments and withdrawals. The eco.wallet handles all cashback internally, avoiding transaction costs and keeping accounting clean.

Anti-Fraud System

LayerMethodPrevents
QR UniqueToken per packaging, single-useReusing same QR code
Photo + HashSHA256 image fingerprintDuplicate photos, screenshots
Time DelayMin hours between purchase and recycleRecycling without buying
Daily LimitMax cashback per user per dayRecycling farms
GPS ZoneValidated against known drop pointsRemote fake validation
Truck ConfirmPhysical pickup by registered truckFalse remote validation

QR Specification

Format: ECO-{YYMMDDHHMM}-{uuid6}

Example: ECO-2604041530-a3f2b1

Structure:
  ECO-          → EcoCupon prefix
  2604041530    → Timestamp (YYMMDDHHMM)
  a3f2b1        → UUID first 6 chars

Cryptographic:
  - SHA256 hash of QR code → stored in qr_hash
  - HMAC-SHA256(qr_code:order_id:product_id, secret) → stored in qr_signature
  - Verified on every scan against system secret
